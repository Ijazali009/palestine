About This Initiative

This application helps identify products, companies, and services that support Israel either directly or indirectly. By boycotting these entities, we can collectively apply economic pressure to end the occupation and human rights violations against Palestinians.

The database includes both Israeli products and international companies that have significant investments in or support for Israel.

BDS Movement

This app supports the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement, a Palestinian-led movement for freedom, justice and equality.

BDS upholds the simple principle that Palestinians are entitled to the same rights as the rest of humanity and calls for:

  • Ending occupation and colonization
  • Full equality for Arab-Palestinian citizens
  • Respecting Palestinian refugees' right to return
